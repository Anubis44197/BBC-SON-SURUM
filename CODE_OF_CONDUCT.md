# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on technical merit and collaboration.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Doxxing, threats, or abusive language
- Deliberate disruption of collaborative work

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to maintainers via GitHub contact channels.
